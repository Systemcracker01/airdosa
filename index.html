The Physics of Crispiness

We solved the moisture problem. Standard delivery makes dosas soggy. AirDosa launches custom drones optimized to deliver the perfect golden crunch.

Batter AI™ Modeling

Our machine learning models constantly analyze ambient pressure, wind shear, and relative humidity across Bengaluru to alter battery temperature and active yeast kinetics during drone transit.

Dosas are cooked mid-flight in carbon-fiber thermal pods. Computer-controlled ventilation forces moist steam out while circulating pre-heated ghee vectors, maintaining a precise crisp-state at 78.4°C.
